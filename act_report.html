Analysis Report on Weratedogs Udacity project¶

WeRateDogs is a twitter account that rates people's dogs with humerous content about the dogs.

this project was worked around over 5000+ tweets by WeRateDogs twitter handle. it comprises of three datasets.

This report communicate the insights and visualizations produced from the projects.

The following are some insights found:

  • most common dog names

  • most common dog breeds

  • tweets with the highest favourite count and retweet count

  • correlation between date tweet was posted, favorite_count and retweet_count

1) Most common dog names tweeted about¶

Base on the data available, there is a total of 772 dog names for a total of 1749 tweets, of the 1749 tweets available 547 tweets didn't have a name for the dogs

the most common dog name is Cooper appearing 11 times, followed by Charlie, lucy, Oliver coming up 10 times

here is a visualization of the top 10 most common dog names

2) Most common dog breeds tweeted about¶

There is a total of 113 breeds available in all 1749 twwets. of the 113 breeds golden retriever occured the most, appearing in over 173 tweets. below is a visualization of the top 10 most common dog breeds

3) tweets with the highest favourite count and retweet count¶

tweet with highest retweet count

The tweet with the highest retweet counts is that of a shih-Tzu breed, not very common as it isnt among the top 10 dog breeds available on this data, the tweet was posted on the 18 june 2016

Tweet with favourite count

4) correlation between date tweet was posted, favorite_count and retweet_count¶

tweet with highest retweet count was posted on june 2016 and tweet with favorite count was posted on january 2017, this led me to confirm if there was a correlation between favorite count, retweet counts and time tweet was posted.
